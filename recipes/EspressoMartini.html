Espresso Martini

An easy to follow Espresso Martini Recipe:

  • Ingredients:
    • Single Espresso
    • 50 ml (1 shot) Kahlua
    • 100ml (2 shots) Vodka
    • 3 Coffee Beans
    • Ice
  • Steps:
    1. Fill the cocktail shaker full of ice
    2. Add the Vodka, Kahlua and Espresso
    3. Shake until the cocktail shaker becomes frosted on the outside
    4. Pour into the Martini Glass through a strainer
    5. Add 3 coffee beans on the completed drink
