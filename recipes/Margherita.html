Margherita

An easy to follow Margherita Recipe:

  • Ingredients:
    • Ice
    • 2 to 3 limes
    • 50 ml of Tequila (2 shots)
    • 25 ml of Triple Sec (1 shot)
    • Salt
  • Steps:
  1. Put the ice in
  2. Squeeze the limes to make 2 shots
  3. Add x shots of vodka
  4. Shake until the shaker is frosted on the outside
  5. Salt the Rim
