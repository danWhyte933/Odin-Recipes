Old Fashioned

An easy to follow Old Fashioned Recipe

  • Ingredients:
    • Ice
    • 1-2 dashes of Angostura bitters
    • Splash of water
    • 60ml of Scoth whisky or bourbon
    • Soda Water
    • orange peel
    • 2 tsp granulated sugar
  • Steps:
  1. Put the sugar, bitters and water in a small tumbler
  2. Mix until the sugar dissolves if using granulated
  3. Fill your glass with ice and stir in the whisky
  4. Add a splash of soda water if you like and mix
  5. Garnish with the orange
